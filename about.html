About Us

At TRI Services Solutions, we specialize in upgrading the comfort and efficiency of your home with modern hot water systems and air conditioning solutions. Our mission is to help you enjoy year-round comfort while reducing energy use, lowering utility costs, and increasing the overall value of your property.

With Government Rebates supported by the Victorian Energy Upgrades (VEU) Program, we make energy-efficient upgrades more accessible and affordable.

With a strong focus on hot water system replacements and air conditioning upgrades, we bring expert knowledge, quality workmanship, and a commitment to hassle-free service. Whether you're replacing an outdated system or upgrading to a more energy-efficient model, we ensure a smooth, clean, and professional installation — with minimal disruption to your daily routine.

Our Expertise Includes:

  • Hot Water System Replacements (Electric, Gas, and Heat Pump)
  • Energy-Efficient Hot Water Upgrades
  • Split System & Ducted Air Conditioning Installations
  • AC System Upgrades & Replacements
  • Smart Climate Control Solutions
  • Residential & Small Commercial Projects

At TRI Services Solutions, we believe upgrading your home's systems should be simple and stress-free. That’s why we handle everything — from system selection to installation and aftercare — with transparency, precision, and a focus on long-term performance.
